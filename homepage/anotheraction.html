THE LIFE

SO HE HAD HIS TIME.

SO THE HOUND TOLD AT LENGTH TO THE WIND, TOLD ON LIPS USED BY THE COLD. WHY SO MANY STARS UPON HIS BACK.

BOUND TOO LONG TO THE WANDERINGS OF A TREE CUT OUT FROM THE ROOTS OF THE HEART?

WHY DISTANCE IN HIS VOICE WHEN HE SPOKE OF DISPELLED VISIONS, BODIES NAILED AGAINST A SONG NEVER HEARD, A CRY CLOUDING THE SHARP MIRRORS OF A SEA DUMB

AT THE BOTTOM, FOREIGN TO THE EYE?

WHY NO ECHOES FOR HIM, TO GREET A KING WHO CAME HOME FROM AFAR, A RING CLOSING (PERHAPS NOT) A MERE ROUND?

ROUND OCEAN. ALIEN SHORES, COVER HIS HEAD WITH OBLIVION AND SALT. A KING'S SAD, UNREAL HOMECOMING;

OLD SEA OF EVER, VAGUE CRADLE, MERE PART OF NO CERTAIN WHOLE, OF HIS DREAM.
